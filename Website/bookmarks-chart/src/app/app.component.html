BookMarks Database Searcher

BookMarks is a critical aggregate website launched by LitHub in 2016, analogous to Rotten Tomatoes or Metacritic for books. Reviews from book critics across the web are given a score ranging from 1 to 4 (1 for Pan, 2 for Mixed, 3 for Positive, and 4 for Rave) and compiled into one aggregate score. However, the website is not easily searchable by average score for different genres or years. The tool below uses a database scraped from BookMarks to provide a searchable archive of aggregate scores for the purpose of research or curiosity.
